## Description
<!-- description:start -->
<p><span style="font-size: 18px;">You are given a <strong>cubic dice</strong> with <strong>6</strong> faces. All the individual faces have a number printed on them. The numbers are in the range of <strong>1 to 6</strong>, like any ordinary dice. You will be provided with a face of this cube, your task is to guess the number on the opposite face of the cube.</span></p>
