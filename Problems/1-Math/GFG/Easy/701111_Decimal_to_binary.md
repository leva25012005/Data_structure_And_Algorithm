## Examples

<pre>
<strong>Input:</strong> n = 12
<strong>Output:</strong> 1100
<strong>Explanation:</strong> The binary representation of 12 is "1100", since 12 = 1×23 + 1×22 + 0×21 + 0×20

<pre>
<strong>Input:</strong> n = 33
<strong>Output:</strong> 100001
